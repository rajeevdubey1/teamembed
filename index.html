Why Launch a Marketplace?

Banks, super-apps, and digital wallets constantly seek ways to enhance customer value by offering non-banking services. embed's B2B2C platform simplifies the process of accessing popular categories.

Offer services customers typically obtain elsewhere to strengthen loyalty.

Embed's service providers have large customer bases and social media followings, which can be leveraged to promote your marketplace, increase exposure, and attract new customers.

A marketplace can significantly increase the time customers spend on your app, as they have more reasons to return and explore new offerings.

In today's data-driven world, understanding your customers is invaluable. Embed's platform provides real-time data analysis through a comprehensive dashboard, enabling you to tailor your core offerings to customer habits.
